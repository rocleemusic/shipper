# Template — Pitch Deck Outline (8 slides)

Eight slides on the brand's archetypes (RDS: title, section, comparison, bigquote, data, code). One idea per slide. Mixed-weight headline — one emphasized phrase. The **comparison** slide carries the duel; the **data** slide carries the ROI / decision math. Feed this outline to the visual tool (see `reference/tooling.md`); this is the plan, not the rendered deck.
